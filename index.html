Hacks for booking cheap flight tickets

Why travel for more, when you have the option to travel for a lesser cost? In this beautiful world where travel options are plenty, it is imperative that all of as get bit by the travel bug and flying to places you desire has become a lot easier. But only some get the knack of booking cheap flights, and this write-up is intended to make you one among those!

Choose the best booking sites

Airfare search engines play a major role in booking cheap flights. Travel booking sites or mobile applications help you grab great deals on fright fares. With a very friendly user interface and a search engine that is one of the best, Skyscanner is your go-to place for great flight deals.

When to book?

It is a given that you cannot always have a pre-planned trip. However, if you are fortunate enough to know the dates, or if you have a flexible schedule, January is the best time to book flight tickets. Also, booking on weekends can end up in you paying more for your next trip.

Things to do right away!

If you are a frequent traveller, it is best that you sign up for cheap fare alerts and subscribe for airline newsletters. This is one way to know the price fluctuations.

Here is an awesome hack that'll help you travel cheaper! Some places have more than one airport. Check for flight tickets to alternative airports and you never know, you can travel way cheaper! Also, clearing caches or checking flight ticket fares through a private window help in grabbing discounted rates.

Be flexible

Being flexible is one of the key pointers to travel for less. There are two things that you need to be flexible about, the dates and the timings. You are gifted if you are open with the destination as well because choosing destinations based on price can do wonders to reduce your trip cost!

Five Ways to Travel in cheap in Luxury (Business class)

Book early

Booking early has a lot of advantages, the primitive and the most important being reduced fares. If you plan your trip early, chances are that you can fly in the business class at a cheaper cost!

Subscribe and follow

Subscribe to the airline newsletters as the early bird deals, and upcoming business class deals are offered primarily to the subscribers. Also, following the airlines page on social media can help you grab deals on your next luxury flight trip!

Join loyalty programs

Loyalty programs that airlines like Virgin and Quantas offer are great ways to collect points and fly in the business class. You can also try to secure airline branded credit cards to grab great deals on the business class airfare.

Search engines

Search engines like Skyscanner and Expedia help you browse for cheaper business class tickets. If flexible with travel dates, these booking sites can work wonders for you.

Ask!

Chances are that you can get a free upgrade if you politely ask for it on the day of travel! If you travel solo, there are more chances that you can travel in the business class by paying the economy class fare!
